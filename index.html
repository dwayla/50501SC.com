About 50501SC.com: Empowering Your Action in South Carolina

50501SC.com is part of the broader decentralized 50501 movement—inclusive, non-violent, and focused on resolving conflict peacefully. Here at 50501SC.com, we’re independent but aligned with the goal of upholding the Constitution and fighting executive overreach.

We believe in the power of people to act with integrity when they’re trusted and informed. That’s why we don’t micromanage. As long as actions fit our core values—defending rights, non-violence, and integrity—we’re all in to support each other.

While other groups in SC may have similar names, 50501SC.com is its own thing. We’re about connecting and empowering people statewide with tools, info, and solidarity—no gatekeeping.

If you’re ready to stand up for our rights and values, with courage and compassion, you’re in the right place. We love you and we are glad you’re here.
